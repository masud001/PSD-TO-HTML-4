The Tapestry Condo is a development owned by City Development Limits. The land was released in the first half of 2017 by the Government Land Sales Programme and streches across 233,767 sq. ft. The overall plan is to build 800 units to fill the plot of land. Even though the location of land is not near any MRT transport, it is still considered to be an extremely valuable market, as shown by sales in previous developments (The Alps Residemces and The Santorini).
